# GPT Image Brief QA

- Purpose, audience, final format, aspect ratio, and dimensions are explicit.
- Brief references NERO design tokens instead of vague color taste.
- Safe area for later text/chart overlay is specified.
- Forbidden elements include exact text, financial numbers, tables, chart labels, regulatory wording, private evidence, and credentials.
- Generated output is treated as visual material only, not evidence.
- Exact text, numbers, chart labels, and source notes are added by Satori/Sharp, PptxGenJS, or Remotion after image generation.
- Final composite passes visual QA for dimensions, text fit, contrast, chart readability, and source-note visibility.
