# NERO PPT Master Guidance

Use this guidance for native PPTX decks generated by PptxGenJS.

## Slide Types

- `cover`: title, subtitle, period, source boundary, optional generated background素材.
- `section`: chapter number, chapter title, one-sentence scope.
- `data`: title, chart/table area, source note, footnote boundary.
- `matrix`: two-dimensional comparison with explicit dimensions and source note.
- `conclusion`: first-line judgment, evidence bullets, risks or assumptions.

## Geometry

- 16:9 wide layout.
- Top title block starts near `0.38in`.
- Left margin defaults to `0.55in`.
- Footer line uses consistent y-position near `6.86in`.

## Rules

- Exact text, figures, tables, and chart labels must be PPTX objects.
- Generated images are background or supporting visual素材 only.
- Tables need readable headers, enough cell padding, and aligned numeric columns.
