# Visual Scorecard QA

- Scorecard totals 100 points.
- Criteria include route fit, brand/tokens, layout, hierarchy, evidence, charts, output QA, and gpt-image-2 boundary.
- Scoring script rejects missing or out-of-range criterion scores.
- `pass`, `review`, and `fail` thresholds are explicit.
- Evidence problems cannot be overridden by a high visual score.
